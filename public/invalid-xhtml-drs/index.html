When We Are Closed

Health services are currently very busy. Please help the NHS help you by using the most appropriate NHS service if you need help.  The Practice will be closed on Bank Holidays:  Monday 2nd May and Thursday 2nd June and Friday 3rd June.  We expect our phone lines to be very busy when we re-open.  Please consider using other services listed below if appropriate.

Local pharmacies are open over the weekend and can provide expert help and advice.  Please always consider seeking advice from your Pharmacy.

Minor Injury Units and Urgent Treatment Centres are also open over the weekend - see details below.

Please only go to A&E or Emergency Departments if it is a life-saving emergency, like a suspected stroke or heart attack or severe bleeding.

NHS111 can help you get to the right service and they can link you in with out of hours GP services, walk-in-centres and other services.

Please consider attending the Urgent Treatment Centres if appropriate.  Follow the links below for more information:

When We Are Closed

Out of hours emergency cover is managed by Harmoni West Sussex.

If you require urgent medical attention when the surgery is closed, please telephone 01444 412280 to be reminded of their telephone number or call 111.

The doctor on call may arrange to meet you at the designated centre (Princess Royal, Haywards Heath) or visit you at home if he or she feels it to be appropriate. Medical advice may also be given by a doctor or triage nurse over the telephone.

 If you need medical help for non-life threatening conditions when we are closed, telephone NHS 111 or visit www.111.nhs.uk or download the LIVI app or visit your local pharmacy. 

Alternatively you can go to Crawley Urgent Treatment Centre, Crawley Hospital, West Green Drive, Crawley, RH11 7DH. 01293 600300 ext 4141 (24 hours, 365 days a year, including Christmas Day).

Minor Injuries Unit, Uckfield Community Hospital, Framfield Road, Uckfield, TN22 5AW. 01825 745030 (8 am - 8 pm, 7 days a week, open Christmas Day 9 am - 3 pm)

Minor Injuries Unit, Lewes Victoria Hospital, Nevill Road, Lewes, BN7 1PE. 01273 474153
